THE PACIFIST (STANDALONE)

This mod is no longer updated standalone. It's now in the Renegade's Gun Shop weapon pack.

First release: September 5, 2021

Latest release (Standalone): September 6, 2021

The second addon and the first weapon addon that is a 7mm medium machine gun. This was me making an attempt to make the M60 into Hideous Destructor, but ended up being a kindof a different weapon, unaware that there is a 7mm lmg from Potetobloke's Bangers and Mash weapon pack. The safe/fire firemode selector is the result of me trying to make the gun semi/auto but ended up having the semi mode to not fire at all, and, I just leave it there for whatever reason 2021 me had in store.

When I was making my weapon pack, I thought of reworking this as an automatic grenade launcher since nobody has made one yet. Not to mention that the M60 actually exists now in Bangers and Mash.

Loadout codes for the gun and mag are PAC and 7MG respectively.

In-hand gun sprite made by Teesefy, based on Doom Eternal's Heavy Cannon by id Software, pickup and mag sprites made by me.

Get it from here, this entry is here for the sake of archiving things.

NOTE: This build is a machine gun, the currently maintained one is an automatic grenade launcher.
